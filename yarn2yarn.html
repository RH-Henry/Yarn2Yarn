Convert • Adapt • Knit

Make any pattern fit any yarn — instantly

Yarn 2 Yarn helps to convert written or graphical knitting patterns to match the yarn and gauge you have on hand. Combine the pattern and the yarn you love without having to use specific ones, be creative and use all your yarns. Generate clear row-by-row instructions, stitch counts, and a printable grid — ready to knit, and create patterns.

  • ●Adapt any pattern — swap yarns instantly without math.
  • ●Share & collaborate — save, export and send a friend-ready version.
  • ●Practical outputs — printable grid, row instructions, and gauge settings.

Why it matters

No more guessing which yarn to buy or wrestling with math. No recalculations and formulas. Keep the design you love — use the yarn you already own. Save money. Perfect for makers who value thrift, creativity, and sharing.

Key features

Automatic conversion

Enter original gauge and target yarn — get new stitch & row counts automatically. Convert the pattern you created or copied. Recreate the clothes you have by mesuring them. Reuse the pattern your beloved pattern over and over with different yarns.

Exportable instructions

Row-by-row text, printable grid or graphical instructions.

Community sharing

Save patterns to share with friends — convert instantly for their yarn.

Intuitive software

Visual editor with adjustable control points and grid snap. If you have any trouble, I will help you.

Design

Create the pattern you want, use the wool you want, or design a motif. You can also copy a piece you love by mesuring the lenghts.

A quick story

In high school, a student wanted to help his mother follow patterns with whatever wool they had at home. Years later, during engineering school, he built Yarn 2 Yarn — a practical tool that removes fastidious maths so families and communities can keep making, together.

Who is this for?

Hobby knitters, small designers, yarn shops and community knitting groups who want to reuse beloved patterns with new yarns. Tech-savvy makers who appreciate clean exports and sharing.
